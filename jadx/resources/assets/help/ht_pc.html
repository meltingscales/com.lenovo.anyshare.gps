How to transfer files between Android devices and PC?

[Send files from PC to Android devices]

On PC

1. Install and launch SHAREit on PC

2. Click Send on home page

3. Select the files you want to send, and click Next

4. Select receiver's avatar in the search page

On Android Devices

Click Receive on home page and wait for the operation complete.(Tip If the avatar on Android devices hasn't been found on PC, please click Connect to PC at the bottom of the searching page on Android device and Open the same network mode.


[Send files from Android devices to PC]

On Android devices

1. Tap Send on Home page

2. Select the files you want to send, and tap Send

3. Choose the receiver's avatar on Select Receiver page.

On PC

1. Install and launch SHAREit on PC

2. Tap Receive on Home page

(Tips If the avatar on PC hasn't been found on Android devices , please find and click Same Wi-Fi Mode at the bottom of the searching page on PC SHAREit and then search the Android device.)
