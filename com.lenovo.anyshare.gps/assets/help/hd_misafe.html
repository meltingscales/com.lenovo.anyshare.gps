Configuring Xiaomi Security Center

Some Xiaomi device users may have incorrect permission settings in the Security Center that will cause SHAREit to be unable to send or receive files normally.


To resolve this issue, follow the steps below

1. Find and open the Xiaomi Security Center

2. Select Permissions -'Permissions'

3. Find and open the permission interface for SHAREit, and then enable Get location info and Change Wi-Fi connectivity.


SHAREit is a safe and convenient transfer tool and we won't access to your privacy through enabled permissions. Please enable the default permissions of SHAREit when installing or make sure the authority of SHAREit in Security Center on your phone.
